Methodology

How we work.

A four-stage method. We use the same four stages for every engagement, regardless of practice — because discipline is the only honest hedge against model risk.

Stage 01
01
Frame
Define the decision the work has to support. No model is fit for a purpose that was never written down.
Output · Decision memo
Stage 02
02
Ingest
Pull data from the sources your decision actually depends on — including the unstructured ones analysts usually skip.
Output · Reproducible pipeline
Stage 03
03
Model
Classical and modern methods, chosen for the question — not for the buzz. Uncertainty is reported, not hidden.
Output · Calibrated forecasts & scenarios
Stage 04
04
Defend
The last mile — documentation, challenge, board-readable narrative. Built to withstand the audit it will eventually face.

Why Oxlon
Three commitments

Why Oxlon.

We do not ship models we would not defend in front of a monetary policy committee — because we have been in that room.

i
Institutional discipline.
Our engineering meets the documentation, governance and reproducibility bar that central banks and regulators apply internally. It is the floor, not the ceiling.
ii
Economics, not novelty.
We combine modern ML with classical macroeconomic method. New techniques are used where they earn their place — never to replace a clearer story with a fancier one.
iii
A bench that has shipped.
Our team has built production AI systems and published macroeconomic research. The work is not delegated to juniors; the senior name on the proposal writes the code.
