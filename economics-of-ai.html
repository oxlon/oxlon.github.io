For the next decade, the macroeconomy will be dominated by a single question: how fast does frontier AI diffuse through work.

Every finance ministry, every central bank, every chief economist will need a defensible answer. Most institutions are not ready to produce one.

We help them get ready — with method, not with posture.

The four questions
we answer.

Each answered with a defensible model, a written methodology, and a principal on the hook for the number.

i.

The productivity dividend.

What fraction of the theoretical productivity gain from LLM adoption is actually being realised at firm and sector level — and which frictions are binding? We build the measurement stack your statistics office wishes it had.

Task-level matching Firm surveys Production fn est.
ii.

The labour-market path.

Which occupations complement frontier AI, which are displaced, and on what timeline. We give labour ministries a scenario range their cabinet can act on, not a single point estimate that ages in six months.

Occupation task-maps Diffusion curves Scenario ranges
iii.

The fiscal implications.

Compute spend, sovereign-cloud economics, depreciation cycles, tax-base effects, and whether a given state should be building, buying or renting its AI capacity over a ten-year horizon.

Compute macro model TCO vs. option value Tax-base sim
iv.

The institutional adoption roadmap.

Where AI should and shouldn’t be trusted inside an institution, sequenced into a programme your team can actually execute — with accountability, audit and the right human in the loop at each step.

Engagement path

From scoping to standing counsel.

A typical engagement runs across four phases over six to nine months. Some clients stay on as standing counsel; many do.

i.
Scoping & diagnostic 90-minute call, followed by a two-week diagnostic to establish the question, data access, and whether the programme is right for you.
2 weeks
Fit memo & proposal
ii.
Method & baseline We agree the methodology, assemble the data pipeline, and estimate the baseline models — with your research team working alongside ours.
6–8 weeks
Methodology note
iii.
Scenarios & recommendations Scenario generation, policy-relevant sensitivity analysis, and a written report suitable for the minister or board.
8–12 weeks
Full report
iv.
Hand-over & counsel We leave you with a model you own and can re-run, and — optionally — a standing monthly call with the principal.
