Common questions

Answers, before the call.

If the answer to your question is here, save the meeting. If not, let’s talk.

Q / 01
Who will actually do the work?
+
A named principal leads every engagement and does meaningful hands-on modelling. Supported by one to three quant analysts. You will never be handed to a junior team while a senior partner watches from a distance.
Q / 02
Do we own the models you build for us?
+
Yes. All code, documentation, parameter estimates and model artefacts belong to you. We deliver an auditable pipeline, not a black box we can withdraw.
Q / 03
How do you handle classified or sensitive data?
+
We work on-prem where required, with cleared personnel and a security architecture reviewed by your CISO. SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified; air-gapped deployment available.
Q / 04
What do you not do?
+
Anything that a large consultancy will do acceptably. If your question can be answered by a PowerPoint deck and a gap analysis, we are the wrong firm. We take on questions that need a model, a defensible methodology, and senior-level authorship.
Q / 05
How do we start?
+
With a 60-minute scoping call with a principal. No charge, no obligation. If we’re the right fit, you receive a short proposal within a week. If not, we’ll tell you and, often, point you somewhere better.
