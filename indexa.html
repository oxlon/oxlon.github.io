Method

How an engagement unfolds.

Every engagement begins with a conversation, not a proposal. We take on work only where we’re confident the result will outperform an internal team or another consultancy.

i.

Scoping call

A 60-minute conversation with a principal to understand the question, your data, and your constraints.

Typically 1 week
ii.

Shaped proposal

A short, un-padded proposal: the question, the method, the deliverable, the fee. No filler, no junior-written slides.

1–2 weeks
iii.

Senior-led work

Every engagement is led by a principal. You will speak directly with the economist doing the modelling.

4–16 weeks
iv.

Handover & hold

We leave you with a model you can audit, a report that names its assumptions, and a standing line to the team.

Indefinite
