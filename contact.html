What happens on the call

Sixty minutes, used well.

A scoping call is not a sales call. It is a working session whose only purpose is to see whether an engagement is worth scoping in writing.

i.

The decision the work has to support.

We start by writing down the actual decision — a board paper, a policy choice, a methodology challenge. If it can’t be written down, we help you write it down.

ii.

The data you have, and the data you don’t.

A quick map of what sources are in scope, what access constraints exist, and where the gaps are. No one needs to send data for the call.

iii.

What has already been tried.

Existing in-house work, previous consultancies, and the internal constraints on taking another swing at the problem. Full context saves a month.

iv.

An honest view from us.

By the end of the call we’ll tell you whether we think we can help, whether someone else is better suited, or whether the problem isn’t yet well-formed.
