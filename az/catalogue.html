Harmonisation

  • Common annual (1995–2024) and monthly (1995–2024) calendars with consistent definitions across the statistical transitions of the 1990s–2000s.
  • Identity reconciliation: the GDP engine reproduces the Ministry's official projection to within rounding.
  • Vintage control: 2025 is an estimate pending final outturns; the source vintage is recorded for every series.
  • Each series carries a passport — source, frequency, unit, definition, coverage and acquisition status.

Indicator passports

The register holds 29 series: 21 held and in use, 6 Ministry-held and awaiting an inter-agency feed, 2 pending publication. The status column carries the same scheme as the Limitations section.
