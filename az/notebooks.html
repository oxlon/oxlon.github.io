Reproducible Notebooks

Seventeen academic-quality, reproducible Jupyter notebooks reproduce every model on this site and in the CAEM dashboard end to end, from the real data through the estimated model to the figures and the forecast, with the mathematics derived in LaTeX. They are the analytical, auditable backbone of the system.

Run in Google Colab
Upload the project folder to Google Drive as MyDrive/azerbaijan-macrofiscal-notebooks/, then open any notebook in Colab, where the first cell mounts Drive, locates the data and applies the house style. No installation or GitHub needed.
