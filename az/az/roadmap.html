Ready now: built, checked and on this site

All / the following are already assembled, validated and demonstrated here — the data controlled, the models estimated, preliminary results, tables and figures produced. They are in hand and ready to be extended, formatted and delivered:

  • A single harmonised dataset, annual 1991–2025 and monthly 1995–2026, cleaned, validated and reconciled to the /ficial figures
  • Every forecast direction modelled and charted: output, inflation, fiscal, external, labour, investment and demography
  • The full econometric suite estimated: error-correction, VAR / SVAR / VECM, Bayesian and large-Bayesian VARs, Kalman state-space and SARIMA
  • The structural models built: New-Keynesian DSGE, input-output and a computable general-equilibrium model
  • The machine-learning and deep-learning suite, backtested out / sample: XGBoost, LightGBM, random forests, LSTM, TFT and Prophet
  • The fiscal and budget engine: revenue by head, the fiscal rule, debt dynamics and sequestration triggers
  • Fan-chart forecasts, oil-price scenarios and the interactive multi-lever simulator
  • Risk heat-map, early-warning panel, KPI scorecard and a data catalogue with indicator passports
  • Preliminary tables, figures, impulse-response and variance-decomposition results, all on this site and all reproducible

The six-month programme

The engagement turns this preview into the full, live system: production-grade models in your environment, the complete data and code, the statutory outputs, and your team trained to run it. The workstreams run in parallel and the whole is delivered within six months / mobilisation.

Figure 25.1. The six-month delivery programme. Workstreams run in parallel; each bar marks the months it is active.
  • Data pipeline and live ingestion (Months 1–2). Live feeds from the Statistics Committee, Tax Service, Customs, Finance Ministry and Central Bank, with schema validation and revision management.
  • Quarterly accounts and structural model (Months 1–3). Acquisition or temporal disaggregation / the quarterly national accounts; estimation / the five-variable structural model.
  • Operational budget engine (M1–M6) (Months 2–4). The six-module legal engine with head-level revenue, sequestration triggers and /ficial submission formats.
  • Production dashboard and statutory outputs (Months 3–5). The executive decision-support dashboard and the clause 3.7-1 submission templates, in your environment.
  • Training, documentation and handover (Months 4–6). On-site and online training; full documentation; peer review; the system handed to your team to own and run.
