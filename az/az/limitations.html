What the higher-frequency feeds add

The annual estimates use about twenty-five observations on the post-2000 window ‚ÄĒ chosen for definitional consistency across the statistical transitions of the 1990s. The quarterly national-accounts feed the MńįńįS integration delivers roughly quadruples the effective sample and enables the five-variable structural model of the technical design, sharpening the impulse responses and resolving the exchange-rate pass-through to a point estimate.

Method notes

  • Estimation sample. The vector autoregression and its variance decomposition rest on twenty-five annual observations; the responses are directionally reliable but the bands are wide, and the decomposition shares should be read as orders of magnitude.
  • Exchange-rate pass-through. Simultaneity between the exchange rate and inflation means the causal pass-through is estimated as a range (about 0.10‚Äď0.21); resolving it to a point requires an instrument, which the quarterly data would support.
  • The scenario simulator. Its channels are proportional and contemporaneous: it omits non-linearities, dynamic feedback and regime changes, and the debt path simply accumulates the implied deficits. It is built for rapid, auditable what-if analysis; the dynamic and structural questions are the province of the vector autoregression and the DSGE.
