Transmission channels
The channels are first-order and documented: the oil price affects output through the oil share and an elasticity of 0.90; the exchange rate affects prices through the estimated pass-through of 0.21; the value-added tax affects demand and revenue; and expenditure operates through a multiplier of 0.45. Each control corresponds to a coefficient reported elsewhere in this document.
