Scenario Analysis

The oil price is treated as a scenario parameter rather than a forecast. Three statutory paths, and a continuous sensitivity surface, are propagated to output, oil-related revenue and inflation.

The three scenarios

USD 65
baseline
−15%
conservative
−30%
stress

The calibration follows the established practice: the conservative path corresponds to approximately one standard deviation of annual oil-price changes, and the stress path to the experience of 2015–16.
