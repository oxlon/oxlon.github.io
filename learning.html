Curriculum
02

What you’ll actually learn.

A composed curriculum. Not a menu of buzzwords. Every module is taught by someone who has shipped the underlying technology into production.

i.

What a large model is, exactly.

Architecture, training, evaluation, cost curves. Not from analogy — from the maths and the numbers. By the end, you can read a model card.

2 days
ii.

Where AI fails (and how to see it coming).

Hallucination, brittleness, distribution shift, adversarial inputs. A working taxonomy of failure modes — with institutional cases worth learning from.

1.5 days
iii.

The economics of frontier AI.

Compute, scaling, diffusion, productivity. The macro-economics of the present decade, at the level your chief economist will question you on.

2 days
iv.

Institutional adoption, done properly.

Governance, risk frameworks, human-in-the-loop design. A blueprint for introducing AI into a serious institution without later regret.

2 days
v.

A live project from your desk.

You bring one real question from your institution. You leave with a defensible plan, co-authored with faculty, to put on a board’s agenda.
